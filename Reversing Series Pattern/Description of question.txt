Reversing Series Pattern

Print the following pattern for the given number of rows.
Pattern for N = 5
1 
3 2 
4 5 6 
10 9 8 7 
11 12 13 14 15
Input format : N (Total no. of rows)

Output format : Pattern in N lines

Sample Input :
7
Sample Output :
1
3 2
4 5 6
10 9 8 7
11 12 13 14 15
21 20 19 18 17 16
22 23 24 25 26 27 28
